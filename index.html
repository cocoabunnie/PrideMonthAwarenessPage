WELCOME TO THE PRIDE PAGE!

Hi! Thank you so much for visiting our page! We decided to make this page in honor of the first of June, which marks the start of Pride Month, where we honor all of those who are a part of the LGBTQ+ community, and the dark history and many struggles they've had to overcome to be loved, understood, and accepted within our society. We're very excited you're willing to fully understand and get more educated about the LGBTQIA communtiy and the issues they face within our society. Stay a while, everyone is welcome here! :)

What Is The LGBTQ+ Community?

LGBTQ stands for Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer, and the "+" in the end is to recognize others who may not fall under these categories (like asexual, alliences, or intersex) but are still welcome. The community was created to liberate and empower those who identify within these sexualities and give them a space where they can feel welcome, safe, and accepted to express themselves freely in a world that typically doesn't enable them to do so.

What Is The Problem?

Historically, the LGBTQ+ community has always faced discrimination, prejudice, and persecution within our society. Although we are making great strides in moving past this serious issue, it's more than clear we have a long way to go.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to help raise awareness about how you (our beautiful reader!) can help move our society a step closer to a LGBTQ+ friendly community. We have multiple links here where you can donate, and you can learn more about the LGBTQ+ community in the About section of our page.
